We are creating persistent volume and letting K8s know. Again, we are creating Persistent volume claim and letting know Kubernetes and these both will be mapped based on labels.


Persistent volume
Script that we ran in the call
apiVersion: v1
kind: PersistentVolume
metadata:
  name: nginx-pv

spec:
  capacity:
    storage: 1Gi
  accessModes: 
    - ReadWriteOnce
  hostPath:
    path: /data/nginx
---
apiVersion: v1
kind: PersistentVolumeClaim
metadata:
  name: nginx-pvc

spec:
  resources:
    requests:
      storage: 1Gi
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
---
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: nginx-deployment

spec:
  replicas: 4
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: my-app
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: my-app
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: nginx
        image: nginx:latest
        ports: 
        - containerPort: 80
        volumeMounts:
        - mountPath: /usr/share/nginx/html
          name: nginx-storage-label
      volumes:
      - name: nginx-storage-label
        persistentVolumeClaim:
          claimName: nginx-pvc
---
apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
  name: nginx-service

spec:
  type: nodePort
  ports: 
    - port: 80
  selector:
    app: my-app
